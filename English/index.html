About Bait Albahar

Established in 2016, Bait AlBahar is a leading real estate company in Ajman. We specialize in private property management, offering meticulously designed furnished apartments that reflect modern luxury. With expertise in real estate development, land acquisition, and construction, we hold an excellent classification and a coveted license from the Ajman Land and Property Department. Our commitment to quality and transparency is evident through our registration with Ajman Municipality and exclusive access to certify lease contracts. Experience an unmatched blend of contemporary design, prime locations, and exceptional service with Bait AlBahar.
